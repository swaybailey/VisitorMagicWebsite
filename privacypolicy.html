Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2/26/2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Visitor Magic! This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

Visitor Magic does not collect any personal data beyond what is entered by the user. All visitor data is stored securely in your own Google Sheets, and we do not have access to it.

3. How We Use Information

Your data is only used to facilitate visitor follow-ups within the app. We do not sell, share, or store your data on external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Visitor Magic integrates with Google Forms and Google Sheets, and their respective privacy policies apply. We do not control or manage the security of these services.

5. Security

We prioritize the security of your information by ensuring data remains on your Google account. Always use secure login credentials to protect your data.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
