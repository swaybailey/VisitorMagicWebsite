Privacy Policy for Visitor Magic

Effective Date: 2/26/2025

1. Information Collection and Use

The App collects visitor information entered by the user or retrieved from an external CSV source. However:

  • No data is stored by the App unless the user explicitly chooses to save visitor information on their own device.
  • The App does not transfer, transmit, or share any collected data with the app creator, third parties, or external servers.

2. Data Storage

Visitor information is only stored locally on the user’s device when the user chooses to save it.

The App does not automatically retain visitor data or store information beyond the user’s control.

The only information retained after closing the App is the address of the stored sheet from which data is fetched.

3. Data Sharing

The App does not send any collected data to the developer, third parties, or external services.

All data remains private to the user and is not accessed by anyone outside of the user’s direct control.

4. User Control

Users have full control over the data they input and save.

Users can delete any stored visitor information at any time through their device.

5. Security

Since the App does not transmit or store user data externally, risks related to data breaches or unauthorized access are minimized.

Users are responsible for securing any stored data on their devices.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in functionality or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted within the App or on the official Visitor Magic website.
