    Visitor Magic is a simple, low-cost solution designed for churches, ministries, and small businesses to connect with visitors instantly. Developed over years in a local church, it fills the gap for a fast, personal follow-up system.

    How It Works:

    1. Visitors fill out a digital card via QR code, tablet, or kiosk using Google Forms.

    2. Their information is stored in Google Sheets (free and reliable).

    3. Open the app, see the days visitors, and send a personalized message in three taps:

      • Open the app

      • Select a visitor

      • Send the message

    Thats it! You can also import visitors from the last 30 days into your contacts in just two taps.

    Why Choose Visitor Magic?

    • Budget-friendly A one-time purchase of only $5. No subscriptions.

    • Customizable Use our free templates and add any extra questions.

    • Fast & Reliable Uses Googles proven Forms and Sheets for data storage.


    Get Started Today

    1. Download the free templates and set up your visitor card.

    2. Grab the app and see how easy it is to follow up with visitors!
