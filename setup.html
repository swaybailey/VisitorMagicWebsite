Visitor Magic Setup

Welcome to the setup page. Please follow these simple steps to get everything ready.

Step 1: Download the Sheet Template

Begin by downloading the Sheet Template. Once you've opened it, create your own copy by selecting File > Make a copy. Then, adjust the sharing settings to "Anyone with the link" so step is crucial. Make sure to copy the link and save it in a file on your mobile phone for easy access later (this is how the app will find your visitors).

Download Sheet Template

Step 2: Customize the Form Template

Next, click the Form Template button and make a copy of the form. You can personalize any part of the form, but please keep the existing questions and their order. The app will use these to capture the data.

Feel free to add additional questions to the form, such as:

  • Spouse’s name
  • Age group
  • Children living at home
  • Hobbies and interests
  • How you heard about our ministry

Just be mindful not to overwhelm your visitors with too many questions—keep it under 2 minutes to fill out. The goal is to make it quick and easy without frustrating them.
